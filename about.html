quality coffee

At Doctor Coffee Shop, we believe that a great cup of coffee starts with the best quality beans. That's why we carefully select our coffee beans from the finest sources, and roast them in small batches to bring out their unique flavors and aromas. Our baristas are passionate about crafting each cup of coffee with precision and care, using our state-of-the-art equipment to ensure that every cup is brewed to perfection.
From our espresso shots to our pour-over coffee, we're committed to providing our customers with a delicious and unforgettable coffee experience. Come visit us and taste the difference that quality coffee can make!

Our brick-and-mortar shop is located in the charming village "Al Qaraoun" , where we serve up delicious coffee and pastries daily.
